Cut, Copy, and Paste

  • To cut the information you will first have to highlight the text. Do this by holding down your left mouse button and dragging it over the text you wish to relocate. Look for the icon of the scissors on the tool bar, and click on them. This will cut the text, or make it disappear.

  • To copy the text follow the instructions above, but this time select the copy icon.

  • Navigate back to AbiWord. Place the cursor in the section of the document where you want to locate the text, and select the paste icon.
