Overview

This lesson will introduce you to some frequently used concepts for formatting your text. You will learn how to use AbiWord to manipulate text by changing its appearance or moving it around. You'll also learn how to undo or redo an action. These functions will allow you to use AbiWord to enhance your document and start you on your way to creating more functional documents.

Objectives

After completing this lesson, you will know how to perform the following tasks:

  • Align text (left, right, center, justify)
  • Add text emphasis (bold, italic, underline, and color)
  • Select, cut, copy, paste, and move text
  • Undo and redo an action

If you'd like to move forward and learn how to align your text, select Next. If you'd like to go to a different section within this lesson or to a new lesson, choose from one of the options in the Navigation bar.
